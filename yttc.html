Teacher Training Course Individual Retrat

4 Weeks intensive- Rishikesh

Our Yoga Teachers Training Courses (YTTC) is, without any doubt, a life changing program. It gives a strong foundation through a unique structure, systematic sequence and a superior comprehensive quality of multi dimension study, from a both scientific and spiritual perspective.

Under the expert guidance of highly accomplished and knowledgeable yogis, students will be fully immersed into the practices and philosophy of real yoga.

Rishikesh is considered the 'Yoga Capital of the World' and is a powerful magnet for practitioners and spiritual seekers. It is believed that meditating here brings one closer to attainment of Moksha or Enlightenment, as does a dip in the Ganges, the holy river that flows through it. With its beautiful location, many temples, Rishis and Swamis filling the streets, Rishikesh is the perfect place for a yoga retreat, and for a journey deep within.


This package includes:


  • 200hrs certificate recognized by Yoga Alliance
  • A/c airport transfers from/ to Dehradun
  • 27 nights ensuite private Himalayan accommodation
  • daily schedule (5-8 hours)
  • 3 fresh wholesome Ayurvedic buffets;
  • 2 days off every week for optional daytrips
  • Lectures on yogic philosophy
  • Full in depth yoga curriculum
  • Books, manual, study equipment and cleansing equipment
  • 1 Ayurvedic massage per week
  • Day time Uk support for the duration of the stay.


YTTC Courses in Rishikesh start on the 7th of every month, except April and September when the programme takes place in Nepal instead.

Investment £1500 for full board, teachings, study material and local trips.
