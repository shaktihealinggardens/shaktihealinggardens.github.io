Welcome to Shakti Healing Gardens

Welcome to Shakti Healing Gardens, an oasis of peace and creativity. We specialize in affordable holistic and creative workshops, courses and retreats in Uk and overseas. These take place in highly charged spiritual locations, carefully selected to aid light work and personal development. All our teachers, facilitators and therapists have many years of experience in their respective fields and are fully qualified. We share the intention to create a welcoming and loving environment in which healing and transformation can take place; be it a session, a class, a course or an unforgettable experience abroad, be guaranteed that with us you will always find a safe space where you can discover and embrace the beauty of your own divinity, re-connect with it and find the inspiration you need to develop to your full potential.
