2-3 May 2015 Healing Waters Sanctuary, Glastonbury. UK

Join us for this spring celebration in the heart chakra of Mother Gaia- the ancient Isle of Avalon. Here we celebrate life and renewal as we gather in a sacred circle. It s Beltaine. This time of the Wheel is a very good time to sow the seeds of our intentions. The Earth had been resting and regenerating few months already when the Sun God appeared far at the horizon on Imbolc. His force is now closer to the Earth and She sighs in love, as sensual and fertile as ever. This is also the moment when the veil between us and the plant kingdom is at its thinnest. It s time for magic and fairies, for moonlit gatherings and storytelling, for new beginnings and new projects. Together we will journey through Yoga, Pranayama, meditation, dance, sound healing, art therapy and conscious sleep with the focused intention to prepare the soil for the new seeds, letting go of the old and making space for the new. In lightness and radiance we will work towards projection and manifestation. What do we want to manifest in our lives? How do we create it? As the days unfold, participants will start vibrating at the right frequency to allow their chosen seeds to become reality. The workshop will be hosted by the founder of Shakti Healing Gardens in collaboration with other creative healers. Time will be allocated to socialize and share healing. Below is the progamme for the week end, but please be advised that it might be subject to minor changes.

Saturday


  • 10-11 / Opening circle and meditation
  • 11- 12.30 / Restorative Hatha Yoga
  • 2:30 – 4 / Nature Walk: Connecting to the spirits of Water
  • 4- 6 / Art therapy

Sunday


  • 7-9 / Vedic Fire Ceremony at Shekinashram
  • 10 -11:30 / Kundalini Yoga
  • 11:30-12:30 / Somatic Release
  • 12:30-1:30 / Yoga Nidra and Sound Healing
  • 2:30 – 4 / Nature Walk: Connecting to plant spirits
  • 4- 6 / Art Therapy

Participants can choose to join for one or both days. The taught workshop will end on Sunday the 3rd at 5pm. Lakshmi for 2 days £ 130, £70 for one day, inclusive of whole day workshops, wholesome vegan lunch, teas and water. Please note, accommodation and transportation to and from Glastonbury are not included in the price. Early birds rate of £110 for the both days available on bookings before December the 30th. Special rates on accommodation might be available on bookings before that same date. For details on how to arrange your stay at the Sanctuary please contact 07943931032 at your earliest convenience.
