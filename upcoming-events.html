Spring Equinox Special.Soul Gardening: Sowing Seeds

Saturday 21st March, 11:30am to 5:30pm @ TBC Brixton location, London. Uk

Our spring equinox celebration will fall on a New Moon, an ideal time to clear away any old energy and to cultivate the soil before we sow our intentions for the next cycle, into the new year. The new moon is always a good time to work on projection and manifestation, but with the equinox the energies are amplified. This is also the moment when the veil between us and the plant kingdom is at its thinnest. It s time for magic and fairies, for moonlit gatherings and storytelling, for new beginnings and new projects.

Together we will journey through Yoga, Pranayama, meditation, dance, sound healing, art therapy and conscious sleep with the focused intention to prepare the soil for the new seeds, letting go of the old and making space for the new. In lightness and radiance we will work towards projection and manifestation. What do we want to manifest in our lives? How do we create it?

Our Sowing Seeds workshops are very special as they are hosted by Shakti in collaboration with other guest facilitators, experts in various fields who all come together to offer the most amazing, varied and exciting programmes. Time is always allocated during lunch break for socializing and healing share.

Tantric Valentine’s: Loving the Self.

14&15 February 2015, 11am to 5:30pm @ TBC Brixton location, London. Uk

This workshop is especially designed for singles and for couples. It aims to provide a safe and nourishing space to celebrate the primal love story between One and the Self. Without a healthy and loving attitude towards the Self, in fact, the individual can not really develop his/her full potential as a human being; nor can he or she ever be truly satisfied in relationship with another.

During the day we will work on releasing tension, healing the feminine creative aspect of ourselves in order to create a more balanced, fluid and accepting attitude towards life. One of the most common obstacles we are facing nowadays is the lack of feminine essence, even in women. After decades of having to be competitive, strong and independent in this male oriented world, many of us- women and men - have lost contact with their inner feminine power. Now that the feminine energy (Shakti) is awakening, it is very important for us all to balance our energies and align with this creative divine force to allow the latest evolutionary upgrades to take place. In this occasion we will explore ancient Tantric practices for healing, balancing and deep relaxation; somatic movement to release trauma and blockages from our bodily tissues; Pranayama to restore a deep connection with our breath and Yoga to ground in our bodies; Art therapy techniques to encourage creative thinking and self expression.Below is the progamme for the retreat, but please be advised that it might be subject to minor changes.
