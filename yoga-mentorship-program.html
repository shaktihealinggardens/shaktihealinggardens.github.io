Yoga Mentorship Program London

Our Yoga mentorship programme is designed for those who wish to receive consistent one to one spiritual guidance and support from an experienced teacher. Students will meet with their mentor every other week in person for two hours over a period of three months to start with. A monthly group session will be dedicated to questions and answers and will provide the students with a platform to connect with each other and share experiences. For the duration of the programme the mentor will be truly dedicated to the individual needs of the student and support his/her spiritual development consistently and lovingly. A genuine dedication to Sadhana, truth and self enquiry is an absolute must for this type of programme. The students will be thoroughly observed and assessed in order to determine what type of approach is needed to start the process of purification, preparation and progression through the ladders on Yoga. The teaching will proceed systematically and holistically, taking into account the individual’s learning pace and providing the him/her with a valuable foundation to Yogic science with subjects including but not limited to Pranayama, Hatha Yoga, Yoga Philosophy, Basics of Ayurveda, Yoga Nidra. Students will be encouraged to build their daily practice and to attend workshops and classes that would enhance their experience of the taught subjects and to integrate it with creative practices. They will receive 10% discount on all workshops, retreats and treatments offered by Shakti Healing Gardens for the duration of their course. The start date for this programme is subject to mentor’s availability.

Cost for three months programme:


£300 when paid fully in advance; or 3 monthly instalments of £130

We believe that it is as difficult to find an authentic student as it is to find an authentic teacher nowadays. Interviews will take place to ensure the eligibility of the students before they can be considered for this programme. Please contact us for enquiries.

About Shakti

Shakti is a multi-media artist, healer and spiritual teacher with over a decade experience of working with arts in educational and therapeutic settings. Her keen passion for and dedication to the healing potential of the creative process has lead her to run projects for several charitable organizations in the UK and abroad with the aim to spread awareness about the importance of creativity as a holistic and healing practice. She embraced the path of Yoga in 2009 and studied in Rishikesh, India, under the loving guidance of Gangesha Chaitanya, one of Swami Rama’s direct disciples. Her training included Raj Yoga, traditional Kundalini Yoga, Hatha Yoga, Ashtanga Vinyasa, Yoga Nidra and basics of Ayurveda. She is founder and director of Shakti Healing Gardens.
