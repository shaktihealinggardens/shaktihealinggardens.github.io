Yoga Intensive Group Retreat

2 Weeks – Rishikesh, Indian Himalayas

Rishikesh is considered the 'Yoga Capital of the World' and is a powerful magnet for practitioners and spiritual seekers. It is believed that meditating here brings one closer to attainment of Moksha or Enlightenment, as does a dip in the Ganges, the holy river that flows through it. With its beautiful Himalayan location, many temples, Rishis and Swamis filling the streets, Rishikesh is the perfect place for a yoga retreat, and for a journey deep within.

Under the expert guidance of authentic yogis (belonging to the lineage of Swami Rama, a high calibre enlightened masters of Himalayas), students will be introduced to the real yogic life and to the main concepts of Asanas, Pranayama, Dharana and Dhyana. They will be encouraged to build self discipline and they will naturally develop a positive thinking pattern and a deep love for sadhana (yoga practice), which will lead them to that very precious space of peace and stillness within.

The retreat will include lessons on Yogic Philosophy, Yoga Anatomy, Alignment, Mantra and yogic cleaning practices; three wholesome Ayurvedic meals per day.



This package includes:


  • Airport transfer to and from Dehradun
  • 13 nights private accommodation
  • daily programme of hatha yoga, meditation, pranayama, anatomy (5-7 hours)
  • 3 fresh wholesome Ayurvedic buffet daily
  • 1 personalized spiritual coaching session per week
  • lectures on yogic philosophy
  • three Ayurvedic treatments
  • three Energy Healing sessions
  • Daytrip to Haridwar and two optional local excursions

Dates: 7- 20 August 2015







Investment £1300 or £1075 when booking before the 31st January 2015. Extra treatments can be arranged in loco for an extra charge.
