Detox Intensive: Ayurveda and Yoga

4 Weeks Tailored Individual Retreat

Ayurveda is an ancient system of Natural Medicine that originated in Kerala thousands of years ago. The first written record can be found in the Vedas, the holy texts of Hinduism. More than a mere system of treating illness, Ayurveda is a science of life (Ayur = life, Veda = knowledge). Recognizing that human beings are part of Nature, Ayurveda describes three fundamental energies that govern our inner and outer environments: Movement, Transformation and Structure. Known in Sanskrit as Vata (Wind), Pitta (Fire), and Kapha (Earth and Water), these primary forces are responsible for the characteristics of our mind and body. Each of us has a unique proportion of these three forces that shapes our nature.

This retreat is designed for people who want to detoxify their body in depth and boost their immune system. The programme will be the same as for the 2 weeks retreat, but due to the longer exposure to the treatments the cleansing will be much deeper. Also, this retreat is suitable for those who want to treat a specific health condition with natural medicine.
